About this home

A well-appointed 15-sleeper crew house in the heart of Langebaan — spacious, sociable and built for groups. With open-plan living, a cosy fireplace and a proper outdoor braai, it's an easy choice for sailing crews, sports teams, extended families and friends travelling together.

Everything you need is close by: Club Mykonos Resort, Laguna Mall, restaurants, the gym and the beach are all within walking distance, so you can leave the car parked and settle into West Coast time.
