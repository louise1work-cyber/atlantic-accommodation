About us

Local owners who know the coast

Atlantic Accommodation is the holiday-rental side of the Atlantic family — the same owners behind Atlantic Yachting at Club Mykonos, Langebaan. We've been welcoming guests to the West Coast for years.

  • Owner-managed stays. You deal directly with us — no call centres, no middlemen.
  • Prime coastal locations. Langebaan Lagoon and Dolphin Beach — beaches, watersports and restaurants on the doorstep.
  • Insider know-how. Sailing, kite-surfing or a quiet braai — we'll point you to the good stuff.
